About Us

Hello! Goose Logic here. A group of students building what we love. Games. We're inspired by the impact games can have on a person though process and meaning. Like everyone else, we've been effected by the COVID-19 pandemic. Our latest project is Covid-Royale. This is our effort to provide some awareness and togetherness though the medium of a battle royale style game.
